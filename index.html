Kedge

KEDGE Business School is a French private business school of general interest. It was created in 2013 from the merger of two schools: BEM (formerly ESC Bordeaux) and Euromed Management (formerly ESC Marseille).

KEDGE welcomes its students on 11 campuses with a rich and lively student life. 7 are based in France: Kedge Paris, Kedge Bordeaux, Kedge Marseille, Kedge Toulon, but also 3 associated campuses (Avignon, Bastia, Bayonne). 4 campuses are located abroad: 2 in China (Shanghai and Suzhou), 1 in Senegal (Dakar) and 1 in CÃ´te d'Ivoire (Abidjan).

The business school offers initial and continuing education from Bac+2 to Bac+6, particularly in management. 4 generalist programmes stand out: the Grande Ecole Programme, the KEDGE Bachelor, the International BBA and the EBP International. These programmes are ideal for students wishing to acquire a complete management education.

There are also specialised programmes, most of which lead to a Master's degree. Students can take masters in management in the fields of finance, quality, real estate, human resources, international trade or supply chain. Work-study programmes are also available in these fields.

Every year, KEDGE Business School is well positioned in the rankings of the major business schools, for the quality of the studies it offers.
